The AI for Science101 initiative aims to create a comprehensive knowledge platform for the growing AI for Science community. This unique field brings together scientists from diverse backgrounds and creates a new intersection of knowledge. Our team of students, researchers, and practitioners are dedicated to providing informative and accessible content about AI for Science through our blog series. The goal of this series is to introduce and motivate individuals who are interested in learning about this exciting and rapidly advancing field.
We are a community initiative and welcome contributions, please check out our Contribution Guidelines.
